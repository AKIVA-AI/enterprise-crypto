## Risk Management Framework

### Market Risk Management

#### **Value at Risk (VaR) Calculations**
- **Historical VaR:** Based on 1-year historical data
- **Parametric VaR:** Normal distribution assumptions
- **Monte Carlo VaR:** 10,000 simulation scenarios
- **Stress Testing:** Historical crisis scenario analysis

#### **Position Limits**
- **Single Asset Limit:** 10% of portfolio value
- **Sector Limits:** 25% of portfolio value per sector
- **Liquidity Limits:** 50% in highly liquid assets
- **Concentration Limits:** Maximum 5% in any single position

### Operational Risk Management

#### **Business Continuity Planning**
- **Recovery Time Objectives:** 4 hours for critical systems
- **Recovery Point Objectives:** 1 hour data loss tolerance
- **Alternate Site:** Fully configured backup facility
- **Communication Plans:** Stakeholder notification procedures

#### **Technology Risk Management**
- **System Redundancy:** Dual data centers with automatic failover
- **Backup Systems:** Daily backups with weekly testing
- **Cybersecurity:** Multi-layered security controls
- **Third-Party Risk:** Vendor assessment and monitoring
